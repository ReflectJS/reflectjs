Reusable components

You can define parametric custom tags and optionally turn them into full blown components collected in kits. 

<:define tag="app-product" class="app-product"
         :name=""
         :price="">
  [['[[']]name]] &mdash; <span>[['[[']]price]]</span>
</:define>

<app-product :name="Gadget" :price="1$"/>
<app-product :name="Widget" :price="2$"/>
<:define tag="app-product" class="app-product" :name="" :price=""> [[name]] — [[price]]
